About

Haley's Book Club was founded during the pandemic of 2020. What started as a small virtual book club full of internet friends became a 1,000+ person club due to Bookstagram. At the beginning of the month a selection of diverse upcoming releases are featured, members vote for their top choice, and a virtual meeting is held on the final Thursday of the month. If you'd like to join please click on the link at the top of the page that says "join."
